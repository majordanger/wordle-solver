By default the dictionaries from Wordle are used. If you are playing a variant of Wordle and wish to use a different dictionary, either paste it into the field(s) below, or upload from a file. Possible Solutions are the words that could be a Wordle solution. Accepted Guesses are words accepted by Wordle that are valid words in the dictionary, but will never be the solution. The union of solutions and guesses is the complete list of what a valid Wordle guess is.

You can choose to only use the Possible Solutions column when using your own dictionary. As you enter guess information on the Solver tab, the solutions and guesses dictionaries will reduce themselves.

Refresh the page to reset the Solver and Dictionary to defaults.
